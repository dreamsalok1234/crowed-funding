Your property:

This property was built in 1957 and is currently being completely refurbished. It comprises 37 apartments and a commercial unit. Most of the apartments have 1.5 – 2.5 rooms, which is a very sought-after size. Thanks to the complete refurbishment, they will be very well equipped: kitchen appliances by Electrolux, electric blinds and double glazing. The property also has a lift.

Location benefits:

600,000 litres of water plunge into the depths here in summer – per second. The Rhine Falls are one of the most impressive natural phenomena in Switzerland. The neighbouring historic town of Schaffhausen with its imposing “Munot” fort, which has towered over the town since 1589, is just as beautiful.

The historical legacy is lovingly tended in the cantonal capital with 36,000 inhabitants, whereby modernity also has enough room to grow. For example, the head offices of Cilag AG are located in Schaffhausen.
